A Story Forged in Excellence

Amrut Jewellers stands as a testament to dedication, skill, and enduring family values, with a rich history dating back to 1962. Our story began when our founder, Amrut Jewellers, journeyed from Rajkot in Gujarat to Nanded in search of new opportunities. A naturally gifted and highly dedicated goldsmith, quickly gained recognition for his exceptional craftsmanship and meticulous attention to detail in every piece of jewelry he created.

Today, the legacy of Amrut Jewellers continues to thrive. His son diligently carried forward his vision, and now his grandsons proudly manage the family business, upholding the same principles of excellence and integrity. At Amrut Jewellers, trust and the customer’s best interest are at the heart of everything we do. We understand that jewelry often marks life’s most precious moments, and we are committed to providing not just beautiful pieces, but also complete peace of mind. Our long-standing reputation is built on transparency, fair dealings, and a genuine desire to ensure every customer feels valued and confident in their purchase.
